Privacy Policy (Internal Use)

Controller: Morgan Records Management

What we process

  • QuickBooks Online customer, invoice, and payment data necessary to post check payments.
  • PayPal invoice IDs/numbers for matching and cancellation.
  • Deposit PDFs attached to QBO Payments.
  • Operational logs with minimal metadata (timestamps, success/error codes).

Purpose

To automate canceling paid PayPal invoices that correspond to received checks and to create the appropriate QBO payments with the deposit PDF attached.

Storage & retention

  • OAuth tokens are stored securely and rotated per Intuit/PayPal policies.
  • Logs are retained only as needed for reconciliation and troubleshooting.
  • Deposit PDFs are stored within QBO as attachments.

Security

  • HTTPS for all network calls.
  • Least-privilege scopes; access limited to Accounting personnel.

Questions or requests: Dan Craumer.
